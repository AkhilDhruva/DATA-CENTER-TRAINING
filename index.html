    How to use this trainer

    The 4-step dissection

    • 1 · Select component — click any device in the rack (or in the Rack Contents list).
    • 2 · Pull out — critical components slide out of the rack on their rails.
    • 3 · Open chassis — the casing splits apart and fades to a ghost.
    • 4 · Inspect internals — click any internal part in 3D, or use the Internals tab → Inspect.

    Controls

    • Drag to orbit · scroll/pinch to zoom · hover to identify.
    • Power view highlights the A/B feed path; Airflow view highlights blanking panels.
    • Esc closes overlays and exits dissection. Reset view returns to the full rack.

    Specs trace to EIA-310-D / ASHRAE TC9.9; vendor & deployment data reflects the 2026 US AI-infrastructure landscape.
